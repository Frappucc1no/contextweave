# Research Writing Profile

Use this profile when the project is driven by:

- literature review
- research drafting
- claim/evidence iteration
- long-form analytical writing
- source-heavy analytical memo writing

## Primary Risks

Research-writing projects drift when:

- claims move faster than evidence verification
- old conclusions remain in active state after contradictory sources arrive
- section progress is remembered loosely rather than explicitly
- source review history gets mixed with current writing goals
- unverified citations or interpretations are treated as settled support

## What to Emphasize

In this profile, emphasize:

- evidence traceability
- claim stability
- explicit verification gaps
- section-level continuity
- source-driven revisions
- unresolved questions that still require verification

## File Emphasis

- `STORAGE_ROOT/context_brief.md`
  Keep the research goal, audience, scope, and source-of-truth boundaries clear.
  Prefer section keys rather than display headings when reasoning about structure:
  - `mission` for the research objective
  - `audience_stakeholders` for readers, reviewers, or collaborators
  - `scope` for domain, corpus, or question boundaries
  - `source_of_truth` for source and evidence boundaries
  Display headings may render as localized labels such as `Mission` or `项目使命`.

- `STORAGE_ROOT/rolling_summary.md`
  Focus on:
  - `current_state` for current thesis direction and section status
  - `active_judgments` for the current supported interpretive position
  - `risks_open_questions` for unresolved evidence gaps and verification needs
  - `next_step` for the next research, reading, or writing move

- `STORAGE_ROOT/daily_logs/`
  Record meaningful literature discoveries, key judgment reversals, and milestone drafting progress.

## Common Anti-Pattern

Do not turn literature collection into a running dump in the rolling summary.

Do not promote unverified sources or remembered citations into current truth.

If raw source detail grows, keep the current-state file short and preserve milestone evidence separately.
